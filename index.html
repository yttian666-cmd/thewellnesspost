Health Tips
Aim for 30 different plant foods per week for a healthier gut microbiome. Adults need 7-9 hours of sleep per night - consistency matters more than duration. Limit added sugar to less than 25g (6 teaspoons) per day for optimal health. Walking 20 minutes a day can reduce cardiovascular disease risk by 30%. Drink a glass of water before meals - it helps with hydration and portion awareness. Strength training twice a week lowers heart attack risk by up to 44% in women. Take most supplements with plain water - milk can block absorption of iron and zinc. Your liver processes everything you eat. Reducing added sugar protects it long-term.

Students Use Games to Expose Hidden Sugar in Everyday Drinks

At XMUM's "Think Before You Drink" campaign, student organisers turned sugar labels into interactive games - helping young Malaysians see what's really in their cup. The average Malaysian teen consumes 11.5 teaspoons of added sugar daily, nearly half from beverages alone.
